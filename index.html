VTEC Prevention Through Education

VTEC is a serious problem here in Ireland for those of us living in rural areas. A lot of rural homes and especially farms are getting their primary source of water from private wells.

It's these wells that are at risk of being contaminated due to animal runoff and a lack of maintenance. The aim of this animation was to focus on the farming community and try to raise awarness through a way that's educational and hopefully funny.

HPSC Factsheet

The HSPC website has a very useful factsheet about what VTEC. How it can find it's way into the food chain. How you can spot the signs of infection and also protect yourself.

ASFM Investigative Report

The american society for microbiology did an extensive investigation into the behaviour pattern of Escherichia coli O157:H7 and how it behaves in different animals.

Irish-Times E.coli Report

The Irish times reported on an Environmental Protection Agency nationwide studies that reveals that E.coli cases in Ireland are rising yearly. It also speaks about the danger to homes that are connected to private wells.
